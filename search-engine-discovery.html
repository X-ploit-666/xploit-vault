The Operator Toolkit

Search operators are the precision tools of OSINT. They filter the noise of billions of pages to pinpoint the exact file, login, or error message you need.

Operator Matrix: Frequency vs. Precision

site:

Limits results to a specific domain (e.g., site:nasa.gov).

filetype:

Searches for specific files (pdf, xls, docx, sql).

inurl:

Finds strings in the URL address (e.g., inurl:login).

intitle:

Searches within the page title tag.

Google Dorking

"Dorking" combines operators to uncover sensitive data that shouldn't be public. It's a technique used to find "needles in the haystack" of the indexed web.
