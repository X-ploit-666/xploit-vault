The Security Trap

The "Important Security Insight": Just because someone hands you an ID card doesn't mean it belongs to them or hasn't been tampered with.

⚠️ The Impersonation Attack

If a website asks for your email and token but blindly trusts the email without verifying that the token actually belongs to that email...

POST /authenticate
email=victim@email.com
token=[YOUR_VALID_TOKEN]

Result: You are logged in as the victim.

The One-Line Takeaway

"OAuth Auth = using another website to prove who you are...
but if implemented badly, identity can be faked."
