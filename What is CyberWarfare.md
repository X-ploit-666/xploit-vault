Cyberwarfare is the use of information systems as weapons against an opposing force. According to the U.S. Director of National Intelligence (DNI), it is a major threat to national security, combining both offensive strikes to gain advantage and defensive operations to protect critical infrastructure.

# The two major activities of cyberwar
- **CyberAttacks** 
	- Non-kinetic, offensive operations intended to cause physical or electronic damage. These are the most visible forms of cyberwarfare.
	
|       **Primary Intent**       |         **Method**          |            **Example**            |          **Result**           |
| :----------------------------: | :-------------------------: | :-------------------------------: | :---------------------------: |
| Physical or digital <br>Damage | Viruses,<br>System Overides | Disturbing UAV<br>Control Systems | Destabilization <br>Or denial |
- **Cyberespionage**
	- Intrusions into networks to steal sensitive information. This is digital intelligence gathering for strategic gain.

| **Primary Intent** |               **Method**                |           **Example**            |                         **Result**                          |
| :----------------: | :-------------------------------------: | :------------------------------: | :---------------------------------------------------------: |
| Information Theft  | System Intrusion,<br>Data  exfiltration | Stealing military <br>blueprints | Destabilization <br>or denialPolitical <br>or economic gain |
