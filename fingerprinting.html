Fingerprinting Techniques

There isn't just one way to ID a server. Investigators use a blend of four primary techniques to extract information from banners, headers, and content.

1. Banner Grabbing: Analyzing the "Welcome" message from servers.
2. HTTP Headers: Reading the `Server` or `X-Powered-By` fields.
3. Probing: Sending specific requests to trigger unique errors.
4. Page Content: Finding copyright notices or structure clues.
