Client-Side

The Front End: User Experience

Everything that runs inside your browser. It creates the visual interface and handles interactions. If a site is slow or looks broken on mobile, it's often a Front End issue.

The Trinity of Languages

  • HTML Structure: The skeleton (text, buttons).
  • CSS Style: The skin (colors, fonts, layout).
  • JS Behavior: The muscles (logic, movement).
